## My Projects, Short Version

**Rentickle: Payment and Protection Plans**
Advance payment and protection plan choices used to stay hidden until a manual credit check at the end. I moved those decisions upfront, onto the product page and cart, and let people customize per item instead of cart-wide. Result: confirmed orders roughly doubled, and 82% of people opted into the Protection Plan.

**Rentickle: Checkout Redesign**
The old checkout had a 9.8% completion rate and took over 6 minutes. I merged it into a single page, added a clear pricing summary upfront, and cut a billing address step most users didn't need. Result: checkout time dropped 42%, and KYC drop-off fell 48%.

**Rentickle: KYC and Account Redesign**
The account section relied on a broken, email-based document process. I replaced it with a step-by-step KYC flow with instant e-KYC and a manual fallback, and reorganized order info into clear tabs. This removed the biggest manual bottleneck in the whole flow.

**Canopy**
A live app I built solo using AI-assisted, vibe-coded development. Before you log a purchase, you have to draw something first. That small pause helps interrupt impulse spending. It's shipped and live.

**Lloyds Banking Research**
A research project looking at why people freeze up during big financial decisions, and how banks could connect one-off transfers to longer-term advice.

**Pehel**
A concept app that reimagines job portals for Gen Z, using conversational AI to guide teens through resumes and job discovery.
